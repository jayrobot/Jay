N's Bootlegs

Welcome to my NEW homepage dedicated to bootlegged music! First, I'll tell you a little 'bout myself. My name is Stephen Bass, and as of now I attend the Park School in Baltimore County, MD. The reason I made this page is to meet and trade with other bootleggers from around the world. Please feel Free to E-mail me if you areinterested in trading, or just want to say hi.

Ok, Basicly I'm looking for whatever Rock & Roll Concerts you have. If you think you've got a good DAT, Tape, or CD of practically any RocK Concert I prob'ly want it The only thing i'm not interested in right now are Grateful Dead and Phish concerts--I know these bands are excellent but I really think that you have to go to their concerts to get the full 'experience.'

As you can see, I don't have all that many bootlegs here at the moment. However I will try to keep this list as updated as possible. My rules for trading are pretty simple. I use 60 and 90min. Maxell XL II tapes or TDK 120's. Click here for my trading guideline Do's and Dont's. IF you do not have any bootlegs of your own, but would like something I have, E-mail me and we can arrange something (usually 2 for 1 blank tape trades). You MUST get my approval for a trade before you send me anything. Any random tapes I recieve unexpectedly in the mail I will either keep, or send back to you.
