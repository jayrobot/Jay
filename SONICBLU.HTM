ONLINE.



This is the first attempt at on on-line underground music e-zine. It will feature whatever I feel like writing about, but that will normally be death/black/doom metal, grind, industrial, 80's thrash and twisted acts of depravity, violence and sonic torture. I may even throw in some book and zine reviews as I read a ton of bone-chilling short story fiction in addition to making myself deafer every day. Issue one's contents are non-existent at this time, but feel free to follow links to see where they might take you. I will be working on this as often as I can with my crazy schedule. So check back here often for updates and new features. Also, please e-mail me with suggestions and criticisms so that I may constantly improve this site.
